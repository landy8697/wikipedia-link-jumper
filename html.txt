      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="History">
        History
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Scotia,_Nebraska&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Scotia was founded in the 1870s. A Scottish settler suggested the name
       <a href="/wiki/Scotia" title="Scotia">
        Scotia
       </a>
       , a literary name for
       <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">
        Scotland
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">
        <a href="#cite_note-6">
         [6]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       Scotia was designated the first county seat.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7">
        <a href="#cite_note-7">
         [7]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">

         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Scotia is located at
       <link href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r994658806" rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style"/>
       <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion">
        <a class="external text" href="//geohack.toolforge.org/geohack.php?pagename=Scotia,_Nebraska&amp;params=41_28_1_N_98_42_8_W_type:city">
         <span class="geo-default">
          <span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">
           <span class="latitude">
            41°28′1″N
           </span>
           <span class="longitude">
            98°42′8″W
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="geo-multi-punct">
          ﻿ / ﻿
         </span>
         <span class="geo-nondefault">
          <span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">
           41.46694°N 98.70222°W
          </span>
          <span style="display:none">
           ﻿ /
           <span class="geo">
            41.46694; -98.70222
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </a>
       </span>

       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_8-0">

      </p>
      <p>
       According to the
       <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">
        United States Census Bureau
       </a>
       , the village has a total area of 0.35 square miles (0.91 km
       <sup>
        2
       </sup>
       ), all of it land.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gazetteer_files_9-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-Gazetteer_files-9">

        </a>

       Scotia is located on
       <a href="/wiki/Nebraska_Highway_22" title="Nebraska Highway 22">
        Nebraska Highway 22
       </a>
       (Scotia Avenue).
      </p>

      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">
        2010 census
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Scotia,_Nebraska&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010 census">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       As of the
       <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">
        census
       </a>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wwwcensusgov_2-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-wwwcensusgov-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
       of 2010, there were 318 people, 139 households, and 92 families residing in the village. The
       <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">
        population density
       </a>
       was 908.6 inhabitants per square mile (350.8/km
       <sup>
        2
       </sup>
       ). There were 166 housing units at an average density of 474.3 per square mile (183.1/km
       <sup>
        2
       </sup>
       ). The racial makeup of the village was 98.1%
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">
        White
       </a>
       , 0.3%
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">
        African American
       </a>
       , 0.9%
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">
        Native American
       </a>
       , 0.3% from
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">
        other races
       </a>
       , and 0.3% from two or more races.
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">
        Hispanic
       </a>
       or
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">
        Latino
       </a>
       of any race were 0.6% of the population.
      </p>
      <p>
       There were 139 households, of which 25.2% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 56.8% were
       <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">
        married couples
       </a>
       living together, 5.0% had a female householder with no husband present, 4.3% had a male householder with no wife present, and 33.8% were non-families. 29.5% of all households were made up of individuals, and 15.1% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.29 and the average family size was 2.79.
      </p>
      <p>
       The median age in the village was 42.8 years. 22% of residents were under the age of 18; 6.9% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 22.2% were from 25 to 44; 25.5% were from 45 to 64; and 23.3% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the village was 45.9% male and 54.1% female.
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">
        2000 census
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Scotia,_Nebraska&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2000 census">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       As of the
       <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">
        census
       </a>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_4-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-GR2-4">
         [4]
        </a>
       </sup>
       of 2000, there were 308 people, 140 households, and 88 families residing in the village. The
       <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">
        population density
       </a>
       was 889.0 people per square mile (339.8/km
       <sup>
        2
       </sup>
       ). There were 167 housing units at an average density of 482.0 per square mile (184.2/km
       <sup>
        2
       </sup>
       ). The racial makeup of the village was 94.16%
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">
        White
       </a>
       , 3.90%
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">
        African American
       </a>
       , 0.32% from
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">
        other races
       </a>
       , and 1.62% from two or more races.
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">
        Hispanic
       </a>
       or
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">
        Latino
       </a>
       of any race were 0.65% of the population.
      </p>
      <p>
       There were 140 households, out of which 20.7% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 57.9% were
       <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">
        married couples
       </a>
       living together, 4.3% had a female householder with no husband present, and 37.1% were non-families. 36.4% of all households were made up of individuals, and 25.7% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.20 and the average family size was 2.88.
      </p>
      <p>
       In the village, the population was spread out, with 22.1% under the age of 18, 5.2% from 18 to 24, 20.1% from 25 to 44, 21.1% from 45 to 64, and 31.5% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 49 years. For every 100 females, there were 87.8 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 79.1 males.
      </p>
      <p>
       As of 2000 the median income for a household in the village was $23,750, and the median income for a family was $28,750. Males had a median income of $21,563 versus $15,625 for females. The
       <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">
        per capita income
       </a>
       for the village was $11,871. About 5.8% of families and 7.3% of the population were below the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">
        poverty line
       </a>
       , including 6.9% of those under the age of eighteen and 4.0% of those 65 or over.
      </p>
